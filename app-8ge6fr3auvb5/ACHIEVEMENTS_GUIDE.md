## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How many achievements are there?
**A:** There are currently **40 achievements** to unlock, with 32 fully functional and 8 coming soon.

### Q: Do achievements give rewards?
**A:** Yes! Each achievement awards **50 XP** when unlocked.

### Q: Can I lose achievements?
**A:** No! Once unlocked, achievements are permanent.

### Q: What if my streak breaks?
**A:** Your longest streak is saved, so you can still unlock streak achievements based on your best performance.

### Q: How do I see my progress?
**A:** Visit the Achievements page to see all achievements and your progress toward each one.

### Q: Can I unlock multiple achievements at once?
**A:** Yes! Completing a habit might unlock several achievements simultaneously (e.g., completion + streak + level).

### Q: What's the hardest achievement?
**A:** **Year Champion** (365-day streak) and **Legendary** (2500 completions) are the most challenging.

### Q: What's the easiest achievement?
**A:** **First Step** (1 completion) - just complete your first habit!

### Q: Do deleted habits count?
**A:** Yes! Your total completions and longest streaks are preserved even if you delete habits.

### Q: Can I see other users' achievements?
**A:** Currently, achievements are private. Social features may be added in future updates.
